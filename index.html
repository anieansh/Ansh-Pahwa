I'm a passionate UX/UI Designer with extensive experience in creating user-centered designs that solve real-world problems. My expertise spans across multiple industries including healthcare, fintech, blockchain, and e-commerce.

Currently working as a Senior UX Designer at McKesson, where I lead the user-centered design for the CGAT (Cancer Genomic Analysis and Therapy) therapy ordering portal, enabling oncologists and healthcare providers to efficiently manage personalized cancer treatments.

I hold a Master's degree in Design (User Experience) from Chitkara University and have a strong foundation in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing, with a focus on accessibility and responsive design.
